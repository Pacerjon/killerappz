Privacy Policy

Listworthy is built privacy-first. No accounts, no tracking, and your lists stay in your own iCloud.

Effective: June 13, 2026

Summary

  • Your lists, your iCloud. List data is stored in your personal iCloud via Apple CloudKit. We do not run accounts or store your lists on our servers.
  • Location (Approximate or Precise, your choice) is used only to power the in-app Places search. We do not store or sell your location data.
  • Advertising: the app uses Google AdMob. Ad networks may use device identifiers to show ads. Gold supporters can remove banner ads.
  • Analytics & diagnostics: we use standard platform diagnostics (such as Apple crash logs) to improve stability. No third-party analytics SDKs collect your list content.

Lists & iCloud sync

Your lists and item data are stored in your iCloud account using CloudKit, which enables seamless syncing across your Apple devices. iCloud sync is free for all users. We cannot access your iCloud. Deleting the app may remove local data; iCloud copies persist until removed from iCloud.

Location

Listworthy requests Location permission only if you use Places search. You may choose Approximate or Precise location and can change this anytime in iOS Settings. Location is used to query Apple's map services for nearby results and is not retained by us.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Listworthy may send local notifications at times you schedule. You can disable notifications at any time in Settings.

Advertising & app-ads.txt

We use Google AdMob to display ads. Ad networks may use device identifiers and related information to deliver and measure ads. For authorized sellers, see /app-ads.txt on this domain. Gold supporters can remove banner ads entirely.

Shopping links

Product items may include affiliate links to Amazon and eBay. Tapping a buy button opens that retailer in your browser or app. These links never change the price you pay, and we do not share your information with retailers.

Your controls

  • Location: grant, deny, or switch Approximate/Precise in iOS Settings > Privacy > Location Services.
  • Notifications: enable or disable in Settings > Notifications.
  • Lists: delete data locally and/or from iCloud via iOS Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email listworthy_support@icloud.com.
