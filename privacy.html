Listworthy Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 23, 2025

Thank you for using Listworthy. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Listworthy handles information when you use our app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No account creation required.
  • We do not collect personal identifiers (name, email, phone).
  • We do not track your precise location.
  • Your lists and preferences stay local on your device.

2. Information We Do Access

Search Providers: When you search, Listworthy queries third-party providers (TMDb, TVMaze, Deezer, Google Books, IGDB, ComicVine, RAWG). Search terms are sent directly to providers, not stored by us.

Advertisements: We use Google AdMob for banner ads. AdMob may collect anonymous usage data (like device type, approximate location, or ad interactions). See Google’s Privacy Policy for details.

3. How We Use Information

  • Show you search results from providers.
  • Help you create, rank, and manage lists.
  • Display ads that keep the app free.

We do not sell or share your personal information.

4. Data Storage

  • Your lists, reminders, and settings are stored locally.
  • If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it.

5. Third-Party Services

Listworthy integrates with:

  • TMDb — Movies
  • TVMaze — TV Shows
  • Google Books & Open Library — Books
  • Deezer & MusicBrainz — Music
  • IGDB & RAWG — Games
  • ComicVine — Comics
  • Google AdMob — Advertising

Please review their privacy policies for details.

6. Children’s Privacy

Listworthy is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a new effective date.
