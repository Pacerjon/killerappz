Privacy Policy

Effective:

Summary

  • Your lists, your iCloud. List data is stored in your personal iCloud via Apple CloudKit. We do not run accounts or store your lists on our servers.
  • Location (Approximate or Precise, your choice) is used only to power the in-app Places search. We do not store or sell your location data.
  • Advertising: The app uses Google AdMob. Ad networks may use device identifiers to show ads. See “Advertising & app-ads.txt”.
  • Analytics & diagnostics: We use standard platform diagnostics (e.g., Apple crash logs) to improve stability. No third-party analytics SDKs collect your list content.

Lists & iCloud Sync

Your lists and item data are stored in your iCloud account using CloudKit. This enables seamless syncing across your Apple devices. We cannot access your iCloud. Deleting the app may remove local data; iCloud copies persist until removed from iCloud.

Location

Listworthy requests Location permission only if you use Places search. You may choose Approximate or Precise location and can change this anytime in iOS Settings. Location is used to query Apple’s map services for nearby results and is not retained by us.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Listworthy may send local notifications at times you schedule. You can disable notifications at any time in Settings.

Advertising & app-ads.txt

We use Google AdMob to display ads. Ad networks may use device identifiers and related information to deliver and measure ads. For authorized sellers, see /app-ads.txt on this domain. If you are seeing test ads during development, that is expected while the app is in testing mode.

Your controls

  • Location: grant/deny or switch Approximate/Precise in iOS Settings > Privacy > Location Services.
  • Notifications: enable/disable in Settings > Notifications.
  • Lists: delete data locally and/or from iCloud via iOS Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage.
