Privacy Policy

We build features that respect your data.

Effective date: October 6, 2025

Overview

Listworthy does not collect your name, email, contacts, or other personally identifiable information within the app.

Location Data

Listworthy may request access to your device’s location only to provide nearby place search results (for example, restaurants or venues). Location access is optional. If granted, the app uses your current location at the time of search to show relevant results and does not store or track your location over time.

You can revoke access any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.

iCloud Sync

Your lists and related data are stored securely using Apple’s iCloud. Data is synced between devices signed into the same Apple ID. Storage and transmission are governed by Apple’s iCloud terms and privacy policies.

Advertising (AdMob)

Listworthy uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to deliver and measure advertising. You can learn more about Google’s data practices and controls here: policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

We support Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT). Where applicable, iOS will ask if you allow tracking across apps and websites by other companies; your choice is respected by the app and the ad network.

Analytics

Listworthy does not include third‑party analytics. Basic diagnostics provided by Apple (if you opt in to share with developers) may include crash reports and usage information.

Data Retention

Your content (lists, items, scores) lives in your iCloud account until you delete it or uninstall the app. We do not run our own servers for your content.

Your Choices

  • Control location access in iOS Settings.
  • Manage notifications/reminders in the app and iOS Settings.
  • Delete lists/items in the app at any time.
  • Use Apple’s privacy tools to manage iCloud data.

Contact
