FAQs

Where are my lists stored?

Your lists sync via your iCloud account using CloudKit. We don’t host or view your lists — they live in your iCloud.

Why does the app ask for my location?

Only to power Places search so you can find nearby restaurants, bakeries, venues, and more. You control permission and can choose Approximate or Precise. You can also search manually without granting location.
