FAQs

Where are my lists stored?

Your lists sync via your iCloud account using CloudKit. We don’t host your lists on our servers.

Why does the app ask for my location?

Only to power Places search so you can find nearby restaurants, bakeries, venues, and more. You control permission, and you can choose Approximate or Precise. You can also use Places search by typing a city or area without granting location.

Ads & app‑ads.txt

Listworthy uses Google AdMob for ads. The authorized sellers file is served at /app-ads.txt on this domain.
