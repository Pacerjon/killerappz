Feature Help

  • Places Search: Listworthy uses your device’s location (optional) to find nearby places like restaurants, bakeries, and venues. You can allow or deny this permission anytime in iOS Settings.
  • Ranked Lists: Add scores to items and Listworthy automatically updates your rankings.
  • Reminders: Set reminders on items so you won’t forget what to watch, read, or visit.
  • iCloud Sync: Lists are stored with iCloud so changes appear on all your devices signed into the same Apple ID.
  • Sharing: Share your lists with friends; they’ll see titles, details, and your rankings.

FAQ

Do I have to enable location?
No. Location is optional and only used to power nearby place search when enabled.

Why don’t I see the same lists on my iPad?
Make sure you’re signed into the same Apple ID and that iCloud Drive is enabled for Listworthy.

Can I change notifications?
Yes — manage reminders in the app or in iOS Settings → Notifications.

Ads in the app?
We use Google AdMob to help keep Listworthy sustainable. See our Privacy Policy for details.
