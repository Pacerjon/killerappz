Listworthy

Make lists, add scores, and let the rankings fall where they may.

Download on the App Store

Features

  • Create checklists or ranked lists — from “Best Pizza Toppings” to “Bucket List Trips.”
  • Discover nearby places (restaurants, bakeries, venues, etc.) using your device’s location.
  • Search for movies, shows, music, books, and comics — complete with details and cover art.
  • Add custom scores and watch Listworthy shuffle your rankings.
  • Set reminders for what to watch, read, or visit next.
  • iCloud sync keeps your lists up to date across devices.

Why it’s great

Listworthy is the app to organize and share all your hot takes — and prove once and for all who’s got the best list.

Search a massive library for movies, TV shows, music, books, comics, and places. Discover where to stream your next obsession, create definitive rankings for your favorites, and build the bucket list you’ll actually complete.
