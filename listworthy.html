Listworthy

Track · Rank · Share Favorites

Listworthy is the app to track, rank and share all your favorites. Search a massive library for movies, TV shows, music, books, comics, and places. Discover where to stream your next obsession, create definitive rankings for your favorites, and build the bucket list you’ll actually complete.

Get it on the App Store

Features

  • Create checklists to get things done, or ranked lists to unleash your inner critic.
  • Discover places nearby with built-in location search (restaurants, bakeries, lakes, cities, countries, concert venues, etc.).
  • Add your own scores and watch Listworthy shuffle your rankings to reveal your true favorites.
  • Share your lists with friends and discover new favorites together.
  • Set reminders so you finally watch that movie you swore you’d get to back in 2011.
  • Sync with iCloud to keep your lists up-to-date across all your Apple devices.
