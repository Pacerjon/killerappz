About Listworthy

Create, rank, and share your favorite entertainment lists—fast.

Listworthy lets you build ranked top-tens or simple checklists across your favorite categories. Find items with smart search, drag to rank, and share beautiful, branded images of your lists.

Supported Categories

Movies TV Music Books Games Comics

Features

  • Create checklists or ranked lists — from “Best Pizza Toppings” to “Bucket List Trips.”
  • Discover nearby places like restaurants, bakeries, and venues using your device location.
  • Search for movies, shows, books, and music — complete with streaming links and cover art.
  • Add custom scores to auto-rank your favorites.
  • Set reminders for what to watch, read, or visit next.
  • All your lists are synced securely through iCloud.
